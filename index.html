Logo Significance

This logo is more than just a design, it represents who I am both personally and professionally. The letters come from my own name, with “E” from Jake and “C" &" O” from Cueto, but they also carry a deeper meaning. The “E” stands for the Essence of Christ, reminding me to live a life centered on faith. The “C” represents Compassion, inspiring me to show kindness and care to others. The “O” symbolizes Obedience and Outreach, guiding me to follow God’s will and serve those around me. In my professional journey, “Eco” reflects the values I bring to my work: integrity, empathy, and purpose-driven action. Just as my logo represents a faith-centered life, it also serves as a reminder to approach my career with dedication, compassion, and a commitment to make a positive impact in every project and team I am part of.
